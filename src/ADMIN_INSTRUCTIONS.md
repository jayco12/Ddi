# DDI Website - Admin Instructions

## Getting Started

Welcome to the DDI website admin panel! This guide will help you set up your admin account and manage your website content.

## Creating Your First Admin Account

Since this is a fresh installation, you'll need to create your first admin account:

1. Open your website in a browser
2. Open the browser's Developer Console:
   - Chrome/Edge: Press `F12` or `Ctrl+Shift+J` (Windows) / `Cmd+Option+J` (Mac)
   - Firefox: Press `F12` or `Ctrl+Shift+K` (Windows) / `Cmd+Option+K` (Mac)
   - Safari: Enable Developer menu in Preferences, then press `Cmd+Option+C`

3. In the console, type the following command (replace with your actual credentials):

```javascript
createAdminAccount('admin@ddi.org', 'YourSecurePassword123', 'Admin Name')
```

4. Press Enter. You should see a success message.
5. Now you can click "Admin" in the navigation menu and log in with your credentials.

## Admin Dashboard Features

Once logged in, you'll have access to the following features:

### 1. Overview Tab
- View total page views, blog posts, gallery images, and contact submissions
- See your most recent blog posts

### 2. Blog Management
- **Create New Posts**: Fill in the title, excerpt, content, category, and optional image URL
- **Edit Posts**: Click the "Edit" button on any post to modify it
- **Delete Posts**: Remove posts that are no longer needed
- Posts support multiple paragraphs - just separate them with line breaks

### 3. Gallery Management
- **Upload Images**: Select a file, provide a title and category
- **Delete Images**: Remove images from your gallery
- Images are automatically organized and displayed on the public gallery page

### 4. Analytics
- View page views by page
- Track total website traffic
- Monitor content performance

### 5. Contact Submissions
- View all contact form submissions from website visitors
- See name, email, subject, and message
- Track submission dates

## Content Management Tips

### Blog Posts
- **Categories**: Use consistent categories like "Education", "Projects", "News", "Events"
- **Excerpts**: Write compelling 1-2 sentence summaries
- **Images**: Use high-quality images relevant to your content
- **Content**: Write clear, engaging content that tells your story

### Gallery Images
- **Categories**: Use categories like "Events", "Projects", "Team", "Community"
- **Titles**: Give descriptive titles to your images
- **Quality**: Upload high-resolution images for best display

### Best Practices
1. **Regular Updates**: Post blog updates at least monthly to keep your audience engaged
2. **Image Quality**: Use professional, high-quality photos
3. **Consistent Branding**: Maintain consistent tone and style across all content
4. **Monitor Analytics**: Check your analytics regularly to understand what content resonates
5. **Respond Promptly**: Review contact submissions regularly and respond to inquiries

## Security Notes

1. **Keep Your Credentials Safe**: Never share your admin password
2. **Use Strong Passwords**: Minimum 12 characters with numbers and special characters
3. **Log Out**: Always log out when finished, especially on shared computers
4. **Regular Backups**: Contact your web administrator about backup procedures

## Support

If you need help or encounter any issues:
1. Check the analytics tab to monitor system health
2. Review error messages carefully - they often indicate what went wrong
3. Contact your web developer for technical assistance
